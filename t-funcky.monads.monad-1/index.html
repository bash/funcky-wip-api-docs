Monad<T> Shape

shape Monad<T>

Some general info.

Lazy vs. Eager

Some monads such as IEnumerable<T> and Lazy<T> are lazy while other monads such as Option<TItem> or Either<TLeft, TRight> are eager. The laziness / eagerness is reflected in the implementation of Select and SelectMany.

Query Syntax

Since Funcky's Monads use LINQ's terminology they're also compatible with its query syntax. This is most often useful when working with chained SelectMany calls.

Option<Invitation> CreateInvitationFor(string name)
    => (from person in FindPersonByName(name)
        from membership in FindSecretClubMembership(person)
        select FormatInvitation(person, membership));
